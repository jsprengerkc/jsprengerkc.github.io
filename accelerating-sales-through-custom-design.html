Case Study

Accelerating Sales
Through Custom Design

Enterprise Solutions Coursebuilder

SkillPath’s sales representatives needed a more efficient way to design and present customized training solutions for enterprise clients. The existing process was manual, time-consuming, and lacked the flexibility to adapt in real-time during client discussions. The challenge was to create a collaborative, digital tool that empowered sales teams to build tailored training programs seamlessly alongside their clients.

My Approach

I developed a SharePoint-driven CourseBuilder tool designed to streamline the customization process and enhance collaboration.

Key strategies included:

  • Real-time customization: Sales reps could quickly assemble bespoke training programs with clients during consultations.
  • Visual clarity: Clean, professional layouts and iconography to make course configurations easy to follow and adjust.
  • Interactive experience: User-friendly interface allowing drag-and-drop style selection of courses, topics, and formats.
  • Seamless integration: Built within SharePoint to align with existing enterprise infrastructure and workflows.
The Impact

The interactive tool accelerated the sales cycle for customized training programs, reducing the time from consultation to proposal. Sales representatives were better equipped to demonstrate value, while clients benefited from a more collaborative, transparent, and engaging design process. The result was improved efficiency, stronger client relationships, and increased enterprise conversions.

Tools Used

Adobe Photoshop • Adobe Illustrator • Adobe InDesign • Adobe XD • Microsoft SharePoint • Acrobat Pro • Brackets • HTML5 • CSS • Node • JavaScript
