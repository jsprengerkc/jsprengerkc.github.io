Design Portfolio

Packaging

Product Art for Bourbon
Chocolate Fudge

The challenge for this project was to create an intuitive and engaging online experience for SkillPath's flagship first-time leadership course, specifically to encourage registrations for both in-person and virtual sessions. My task involved developing a web page design featuring a clear information hierarchy and an optimized user experience, simplifying navigation and highlighting the course's key benefits. The goal was to increase conversion rates by providing a seamless journey for aspiring leaders to discover and enroll in essential management training.

Tools: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign

Branding

Crafting the Identity for
The Antler Room

This project addressed the challenge of creating a distinctive and memorable visual identity for The Antler Room, a new, up-and-coming Kansas City restaurant, that would encapsulate its unique ambiance and culinary approach. My collaboration with the owners resulted in a comprehensive logo design that successfully blended sophistication with evocative elements, reflecting the establishment's character. The direct impact was an enhanced brand presence and recognition for the restaurant, providing a strong visual cornerstone for its identity.

Tools: Adobe Illustrator

Web

Your Nutrition Design

The challenge was to design a user-friendly and effective website for a nutrition consultant that would attract and inform potential clients seeking guidance. My solution involved creating a clean, aesthetically pleasing website with intuitive navigation and strategically placed compelling calls to action. The intended impact was to streamline the client acquisition process, making it easy for visitors to find information and connect with the consultant for their nutritional needs.

Tools: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Lightroom, WordPress, Brackets, HTML5, CSS

Book

Visualizing Empathy:
“The Compassionate Geek”

This book design was to visually represent the core message of "The Compassionate Geek" – integrating empathy and human connection within the often technical world of IT. My solution involved a creative design that was both approachable and impactful, conveying the book's unique blend of practical advice and emotional intelligence. The intended impact was to immediately communicate the book's essence to potential readers, drawing them in with a design that highlighted its innovative perspective.

Tools: Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Acrobat Pro

Print

Elevating the Practice: Yoga Teacher
Training Course

This project's aim was to create a visual identity for a yoga studio's teacher training course that embodied the tranquility, growth, and depth of a yoga practice, while also communicating the professional and transformative nature of the training. My solution was the design of a course book cover that artfully combined elements of serenity and strength. The impact was to inspire prospective teachers and clearly reflect the studio's dedication to holistic development, attracting qualified candidates to the program.

Tools: Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Acrobat Pro
